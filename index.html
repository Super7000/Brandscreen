Import

Grab all your existing testimonials from over 30 platforms or just drag in a CSV. Once imported, Senja makes is simple to organize, tag, filter, and share them right away.

Collect

Create and share testimonial forms in no time. Senja handles video and text collection automatically, even transcribing videos you collect natively for easy review.

Manage & Analyze

Tag and sort your testimonials by customer type, product, or sentiment. Use Senja’s smart sentiment analysis to instantly spot what your customers love — and which products or features they keep raving about.

Share

Show off your testimonials anywhere — on your site, social media, or emails — with customizable widgets and Walls of Love.
